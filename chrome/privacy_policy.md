# Privacy Policy for JustCite

**Last updated:** February 2026

## Overview
JustCite is a browser extension designed to help users generate academic citations by extracting metadata from webpages.  
We take your privacy seriously and are committed to being transparent about how data is handled.

**JustCite does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

---

## Data Collection
JustCite **does not collect any personal information**.

Specifically:
- No names
- No email addresses
- No account information
- No browsing history
- No analytics or tracking identifiers

All processing happens **locally in your browser**.

---

## Website Content Access
To generate citations, JustCite reads **publicly available metadata** from the currently active webpage, such as:
- Page title
- Author names (if present)
- Publication date
- DOI or URL
- Structured metadata (Open Graph, JSON-LD, citation meta tags)

This data is:
- Accessed **only when you click the extension**
- Used **solely to generate citations**
- **Never sent off your device**

---

## Data Storage
- Citation data exists only **temporarily in memory**
- Optional user settings (e.g., citation key format preferences) are stored using **browser-local storage**
- No data is synchronized, uploaded, or shared

---

## Third-Party Services
JustCite:
- Does not use external APIs
- Does not communicate with remote servers
- Does not embed third-party trackers or analytics

---

## Permissions Explanation
JustCite requests only the minimum permissions required to function:
- **Active Tab / Content Access** – to extract citation metadata from the current webpage
- **Clipboard Access** – to copy formatted citations on user request

These permissions are used **only when explicitly triggered by the user**.

---

## Data Sharing
JustCite:
- Does not sell data
- Does not share data
- Does not transfer data to third parties

---

## Children’s Privacy
JustCite does not target or knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy
If this Privacy Policy changes, updates will be published in the project repository and reflected in the extension listing.

---

## Contact
If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the project repository.

---

## Summary
JustCite runs entirely in your browser.  
Your data never leaves your device.  
No tracking. No accounts. No analytics.
